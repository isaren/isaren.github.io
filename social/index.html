Social Work

CTO of Boxfriends (2017-)

Boxfriends is a social company which aims to improve the quality of charity in rural areas. We use containers to build a kind of flexible and portable public building and place them in schools for children left behind. The main job for me is to provide the architecture design solution for company’s Internet products (e.g, Internet of things product design, Front-end developments ).

Member of GDG Shanghai (2016-)

Google Developer Groups (GDGs) are for developers who are interested in Google’s developer technology. I was an attendee of Google I/O Extended Beijing 2017 and was a volunteer in Devfest-2016 Shanghai, Devfest-2017 Shanghai (Android related CodeLab).

Campus Department Head of “Student Online” (2016-2017)

“Student Online” is an organization in ShanDong University for students. I was a campus principal in mobile terminal department which aims to develop campus services application in both Android and iOS platform.
